Philosophy

Open Source & Offline-First: The AxelBase Philosophy

Published: November 21, 2025

In an age of data harvesting and cloud dependency, AxelBase takes a different path: radical transparency and total independence.

Our Core Principles

  • 100% Open Source: Full code on GitHub. Audit it. Fork it. Improve it.
  • Offline-First: Works without internet after first load. Save as PWA.
  • No Telemetry: Zero analytics, tracking, or phoning home.
  • No Build Steps: Pure HTML + JS. Runs anywhere.
  • No Accounts: No login. No limits. No data collection.

Why This Matters

When you hash a private key, password, or legal document, you shouldn’t have to trust a company. You should trust math — and code you can read.

You Own Your Tools

Download AxelBase once, save it locally, and use it forever — even if the site goes down. That’s true digital sovereignty.

FAQ

Can I host AxelBase myself?

Yes! Just clone the repo and deploy anywhere — even on a USB drive.

Will you ever add cloud sync?

Never. That would defeat the entire purpose.

The future of privacy isn’t in the cloud. It’s in your hands.
